Who Are We?

In a city like Delhi, where water sustains millions, preserving it is vital. Our water monitoring system, powered by satellite imagery from Sentinel-2 via Google Earth Engine, goes beyond technology—it’s a commitment to safeguarding this precious resource. By ensuring the quality and safety of every drop, we provide hope for families, protect children’s futures, and support the city’s growth. This system represents our dedication to a sustainable Delhi, where water scarcity is a thing of the past, and clean water access is a right for all.
